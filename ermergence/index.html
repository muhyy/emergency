ZARURA TZ

Zarura-Tz, is an Emergency services application for mobile and web application. That combine emergency services such as Fire & Rescue force, Police, Tanesco and Ambulance.
The App is a single click solution that Avoid confusion of service code.

WHY ZARURA TZ
Firstly, To remove misconceptions of the service codes. Secondly, Easy to get access Services with single click action. Thirdly, No need to recall the service codes. Lastly, Provides locations Automatically.
